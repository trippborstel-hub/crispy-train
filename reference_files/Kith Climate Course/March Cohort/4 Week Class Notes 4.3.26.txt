Meeting Purpose

Review weekly assignments and discuss AI-driven analysis techniques.

Key Takeaways

  - Human Steering Drives AI Output: The wide variety of demos from a single prompt proves that human intent and questioning are the primary drivers of AI-generated deliverables, not just the data or model.
  - Embrace a "High-Agency" Stance: Treat the AI as a powerful assistant. Be assertive, question its logic, and demand explanations to refine outputs and prevent "AI indigestion" from overwhelming data.
  - Structure Data for Efficiency: Use structured formats like Supabase or SQLite for deterministic data retrieval, which is faster and more reliable than parsing unstructured files like PDFs or PowerPoints.
  - Manage Token Limits Strategically: To avoid rate limits, use lighter models (Sonnet, Haiku) for simpler tasks like API setup, reserving the powerful but token-heavy Opus for complex coding and analysis.
