What You'll Build
Five deliverables. One model.
  1. 1
    Connect the datasets
    Cross-reference carbon (Week 3) with Supabase financials for every portco. One script, one query layer. No manual joins, no spreadsheet middleware.
  2. 2
    Build the financial exposure model
    For each portco, translate material ESG risks into $/yr exposure. Python, HTML output. At least one worked pipeline end to end (Sterling, Harvest Ridge, or your choice).
  3. 3
    Extend the data where it matters
    Pick at least one portco where biodiversity or water meaningfully extends the picture. Prompt CC to generate baseline data. Iterate. Document the assumptions.
  4. 4
    Produce the materiality matrix
    Per portco or portfolio-level. Probability × $ impact. Upper-right quadrant explained in plain language. Generated by the model, not hand-placed.
  5. 5
    Rank the initiatives
    Top 3 per portco by ROI, payback, and risk avoided. Roll up to a one-page portfolio view Sarah could bring to the Q2 operating review.
