## Key design decisions (rationale in the arc doc)

- **Arc: Pitch it → Ground it → Prove it → Assure it**, plus Week 0 pre-flight and a
  Demo Day in week 5 with the wider practice invited.
- **Fused Kith W3 (Carbon) + W4 (Materiality)** into our Week 3 — the materials already
  frame them as one thought ("the number" → "the meaning").
- **Cut Kith W5 (The App)** to a stretch/alumni topic — dashboard covers the core lesson;
  full apps are the most fragile thing across EY Cursor setups.
- **Promoted assurance to a full week (W4)** — EY's DNA and the program's differentiator.
- **Minimum bar vs. full build** on every assignment — survival mechanism for billable
  crunch weeks.
- **Pods of 4–5**, one per instructor. **Use-case backlog** collected weekly — becomes the
  Phase 3 competition fuel and the evidence base for a Claude Code business case.
