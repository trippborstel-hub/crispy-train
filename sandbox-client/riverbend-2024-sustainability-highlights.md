# Riverbend Beverage Group — 2024 Sustainability Highlights

*Prepared by Riverbend Brand & Communications, March 2024*
*"Refreshing the West, Responsibly"*

---

## Our Commitment

At Riverbend, sustainability isn't a program — it's who we are. That's why we're proud to
announce our boldest commitment yet: **Riverbend Beverage Group will be carbon neutral by
2030.**

## 2024 Highlights

- 🌲 **Carbon neutral by 2030.** Through operational excellence and our partnership in
  the Pacific Timberlands Offset Initiative, Riverbend has charted a path to full carbon
  neutrality across our operations by 2030.*
- 💧 **Water stewardship.** Our bottling operations continue to improve water efficiency
  year over year, honoring the rivers that gave us our name.
- ♻️ **Circular packaging.** Riverbend Packaging Solutions increased recycled content
  across our glass and aluminum lines, keeping materials in use and out of landfills.
- 🚛 **Cleaner miles.** Coldstream Distribution is piloting route optimization to reduce
  fuel consumption across our delivery network.
- ☀️ **Renewable future.** We are evaluating solar installations at multiple facilities
  as part of our clean-energy roadmap.

## By the Numbers

- **100%** — our commitment to a carbon-neutral future
- **65+ years** of family stewardship of the lands and waters of the West
- **4,800** team members who live our values every day

---

\* *Riverbend's carbon neutrality commitment is supported by verified forest carbon
offsets retired through the Pacific Timberlands Offset Initiative (2021 vintage) and
planned operational efficiencies.*
